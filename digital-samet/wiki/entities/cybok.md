# Entity: CyBOK

## Definition
The Cyber Security Body of Knowledge (CyBOK) is a project to codify the foundational and established knowledge in the field of cyber security.

## Characteristics
- Funded by UK National Cyber Security Programme.
- International collaboration of experts.
- Open Government Licence v3.0.

## Structure
Organized into 21 Knowledge Areas (KAs).
