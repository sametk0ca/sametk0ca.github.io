# Zero Trust Maturity Model (CISA 2.0)

- **Source:** [[cisa-zero-trust-2-0]]
- **Tags:** #zero-trust #cisa #governance #modern-security

## Overview
The **Zero Trust Maturity Model (ZTMM) 2.0**, released by CISA in 2024, provides a cross-pillar roadmap for organizations to transition from legacy perimeter security to dynamic Zero Trust Architecture.

## The Five Pillars
1. **Identity:** From password-based to **Phishing-Resistant MFA** and continuous risk-based authentication.
2. **Devices:** Beyond inventory to real-time health monitoring and compliance checks.
3. **Networks:** From flat internal networks to **Micro-segmentation** and mandatory encryption of all traffic.
4. **Applications & Workloads:** Moving to secure development (DevSecOps) and continuous security testing.
5. **Data:** Encryption at rest and in transit with granular, least-privilege access.

## Maturity Stages
- **Traditional:** Static configuration, manual orchestration.
- **Initial:** Some automation and cross-pillar visibility.
- **Advanced:** Integrated across pillars with automated response.
- **Optimal:** Fully dynamic, risk-informed, and automated security posture.
