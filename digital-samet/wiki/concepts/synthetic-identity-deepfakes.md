# Synthetic Identity & Deepfakes (2025-2026)

- **Source:** [[deepfake-service-2025]]
- **Tags:** #deepfake #biometrics #impersonation #modern-threats

## Overview
The industrialization of generative AI has led to **Deepfake-as-a-Service (DFaaS)**, making high-quality synthetic audio and video available to low-skill attackers for identity fraud.

## Key Trends (2026)
- **Live Deepfakes:** Real-time video/audio impersonation during video conferencing (Zoom, Teams).
- **Synthetic Identity Kits:** Turnkey packages sold on dark web markets that include synthetic IDs, voice clones, and face-swapping profiles for ~$5.
- **Voice-Cloning Phishing:** Using as little as 3 seconds of a person's voice to generate convincing phishing calls to family members or corporate finance departments.

## Defensive Technologies
- **Liveness Detection:** Verifying that a biometric sample is from a living human (e.g., blink detection, pulse detection via camera).
- **Behavioral Biometrics:** Monitoring patterns in typing, mouse movement, and device handling to detect non-human or anomalous behavior.
- **PKI-Signed Video:** Cryptographically signing video streams at the source (camera hardware) to ensure integrity.
