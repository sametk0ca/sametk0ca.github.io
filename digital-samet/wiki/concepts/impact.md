# Concept: Impact

## Definition
Impact is the result of a threat exploiting a vulnerability, which has a negative effect on the success of the system objectives.

## Perspectives
- **System View**: Failure of a primary objective (e.g., failure to launch a product).
- **Component View**: Failure of a specific asset or part (e.g., loss of a production machine).
