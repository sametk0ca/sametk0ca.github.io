# Concept: Legal Risk Management

## Overview
Legal risk management involves identifying, assessing, and mitigating the risks of legal action or regulatory penalties before they occur.

## Key Strategies
1. **Identify High-Risk Areas**: Focus on industry-specific regulations (e.g., financial services, medical data).
2. **Due Diligence**: Align investigations with identified risks (e.g., checking foreign laws before expanding services).
3. **Assess Practical Limits**: Consider the reality of territorial enforcement jurisdiction (e.g., can a state actually enforce its laws against your offshore operations?).
4. **Vicarious Liability**: Influence employee behavior through governance and training to reduce the risk of tortious acts.
5. **Evidence Preservation**: Establish forensic standards for collecting and preserving data in anticipation of legal disputes.

## Compliance vs. Ethics
While law sets the minimum standard, ethical behavior often requires going beyond mere compliance to minimize harm and maintain public trust.
