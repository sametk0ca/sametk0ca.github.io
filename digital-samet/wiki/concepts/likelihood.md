# Concept: Likelihood

## Definition
Likelihood represents a measure capturing the degree of possibility that a threat will exploit a vulnerability and produce an undesirable outcome.

## Measurement
- **Qualitative**: Low, Medium, High.
- **Quantitative**: Percentage or numerical scale (e.g., 1-10).
