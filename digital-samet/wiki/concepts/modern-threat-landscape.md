# Modern Threat Landscape (2024-2026)

## Overview
The 2024-2026 threat landscape is dominated by geopolitical conflicts, AI-weaponization, and a shift toward ideology-driven attacks.

## Current Trends (April 2026)
- **Ideology-Driven Hacktivism:** Over 70% of major incidents in Europe are now geopolitical/ideological rather than financial.
- **Deepfake-as-a-Service (DFaaS):** commoditization of impersonation. See: [[synthetic-identity-deepfakes]].
- **Agentic AI Exploits:** Hijacking autonomous agents. See: [[agentic-ai-security]].
- **Virtualization Layer Attacks:** Direct targeting of hypervisors and cloud management stacks to bypass VM-level security.

## Core Pillars of Modern Defense
- **Identity-Centric Security:** Moving beyond network boundaries to Identity (IAM) as the primary security perimeter.
- **Quantum-Resistant Inventory:** Identifying all RSA-2048/ECC-256 assets before the 2030 deprecation deadline.
- **Supply Chain Integrity (SLSA):** Verification of provenance to prevent "upstream" injections.
