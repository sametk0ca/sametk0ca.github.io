# Source: NIST PQC Standards (2024)

Summary of the finalized NIST Post-Quantum Cryptography standards released in August 2024.

## Key Takeaways
- **ML-KEM (FIPS 203):** Primary key exchange mechanism.
- **ML-DSA (FIPS 204):** Primary signature standard.
- **SLH-DSA (FIPS 205):** Backup signature standard (hash-based).
