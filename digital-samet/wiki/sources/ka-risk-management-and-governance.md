# Source: KA Risk Management and Governance

## Summary
This Knowledge Area (KA) explains the fundamental principles of cyber risk assessment and management and their role in risk governance. It covers the definition of risk, the importance of risk assessment and management, and the core components of risk assessment (vulnerability, threat, likelihood, and impact).

## Key Themes
- **Risk Definition**: Grounded in human value (Renn's definition).
- **Risk Assessment**: Analytic and structured process to capture information, perceptions, and evidence.
- **Risk Governance**: Overarching set of ongoing processes and principles for awareness, education, and responsibility.
- **Human Factors**: Security culture, awareness, and the "Just Culture" approach.
- **Methodologies**: Overview of NIST, ISO/IEC 27005, FAIR, Octave Allegro, STRIDE, etc.
