The definitive list of security risks for Large Language Model integrations.

## Key Takeaways
- Prompt Injection remains the #1 risk.
- Insecure Output Handling and Data Poisoning are critical for modern RAG architectures.
- Excessive Agency is a new focus for AI Agents.
