# Source: MITRE ATT&CK v19 & 2026 Updates

Summary of the major structural and thematic updates to the MITRE ATT&CK and ATLAS frameworks as of April 2026.

## Key Takeaways
- **The v19 Split:** Defense Evasion is replaced by **Stealth** and **Impair Defenses**.
- **AI Agent Security:** New focus on context poisoning and memory manipulation in autonomous agents.
- **Unified Metrics:** Introduction of the **TES (Total Evaluation Score)** for security controls.
