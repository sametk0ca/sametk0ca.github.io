# Source: LLM Wiki Concept

- **Date**: 2026-04-11
- **Source**: [[llm-wiki-concept]]

## Summary
This document describes the LLM Wiki pattern: a persistent, incrementally built knowledge base maintained by an LLM.

## Key Takeaways
- Wiki is a persistent artifact, not just RAG.
- Three layers: Raw sources, Wiki pages, Schema (GEMINI.md).
- Operations: Ingest, Query, Lint.
- Special files: index.md and log.md.
